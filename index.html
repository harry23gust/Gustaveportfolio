About Me

Harry is a Sales Engineer at Zoho. Originally from Haiti, he was raised in a family of programmers and artists. He majored in Computer Science, and is passionate about Art, technology and the Human Condition. At Zoho, his current focus is CRM Plus and Zoho One suite. With nearly a decade of experience in technical support and credit card processing, he thoroughly enjoys the challenges Zoho has to offer. Outside of work, he loves Japanese anime, video games and aspires to one day create his own manga series.
